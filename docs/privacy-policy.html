Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Information We Collect

Securaa is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and share information about you when you use our services.

Product Usage Data

  • System logs and performance metrics
  • Configuration settings and preferences
  • Security event data and analytics
  • Integration and API usage patterns

Account Information

  • Contact information (name, email, phone)
  • Organization details
  • User authentication credentials
  • Billing and subscription information

How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services
  • Process transactions and send related information
  • Send technical notices and support messages
  • Detect, investigate, and prevent security incidents
  • Comply with legal obligations

Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties except as described in this policy:

  • With your consent
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect rights, property, or safety
  • With service providers under strict confidentiality

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Data Retention

We retain information only as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your personal information, including:

  • Access to your personal information
  • Correction of inaccurate information
  • Deletion of your information
  • Data portability
  • Objection to processing
