IoC Confidence and Scoring

Confidence Calculation Factors

Source Reliability (40%)

Historical accuracy of the intelligence source

Indicator Age (20%)

Time since first observation, with decay over time

Corroboration (25%)

Number of independent sources reporting the indicator

Context Relevance (15%)

Relevance to your organization's threat landscape

Confidence Levels

High Confidence (80-100%)

Multiple reliable sources, recent activity, high relevance

Automatic Blocking
Medium Confidence (60-79%)

Reliable source or multiple sources with some concerns

Alert & Monitor
Low Confidence (40-59%)

Single source or aging indicators with limited context

Hunt & Investigate
Minimal Confidence (<40%)

Unverified or historical indicators for research purposes

Information sharing classification system

TLP:RED

Not for disclosure, restricted to specific individuals

TLP:AMBER

Limited disclosure, restricted to organization

TLP:GREEN

Limited disclosure, community sharing

TLP:WHITE

Disclosure not limited, public sharing
