TIP - Threat Intelligence Platform

Comprehensive threat intelligence aggregation and analysis platform for enhanced security awareness

Key Features

Global Threat Feeds

Integration with premium and open-source threat intelligence feeds from around the world.

IOC Analysis

Advanced analysis of indicators of compromise with context and attribution information.

Intelligence Sharing

Secure sharing of threat intelligence with trusted partners and industry groups.

Custom Filtering

Advanced filtering and correlation capabilities to focus on relevant threats for your organization.

Integration Ready: TIP seamlessly integrates with SIEM platforms, firewalls, and other security tools to automatically apply threat intelligence.

Supported IOC Types

IOC Type Description Examples
IP Addresses Malicious IP addresses and ranges Command & Control servers, botnets
Domain Names Malicious domains and subdomains Phishing sites, malware distribution
File Hashes MD5, SHA1, SHA256 hashes Malware samples, suspicious files
URLs Malicious URLs and paths Exploit kits, phishing pages
Email Addresses Malicious email addresses Spam sources, phishing campaigns

Threat Intelligence Lifecycle

1

Collection

Gather threat intelligence from multiple sources including commercial feeds, open source intelligence, and internal security events.

2

Processing

Normalize, deduplicate, and enrich threat data with additional context and attribution information.

3

Analysis

Analyze threat patterns, correlate indicators, and assess relevance to your organization's threat landscape.

4

Dissemination

Distribute actionable intelligence to security tools, teams, and stakeholders through automated feeds and reports.

Intelligence Sources

Commercial Feeds

Premium threat intelligence from leading commercial providers with high-fidelity indicators and detailed analysis.

Open Source Intelligence

Community-driven threat intelligence from public repositories, research organizations, and security communities.

Government Sources

Threat intelligence from government agencies, including classified and unclassified threat bulletins.

Internal Intelligence

Organization-specific threat intelligence derived from security incidents, honeypots, and internal analysis.

STIX/TAXII Support

Standards Compliance: Full support for STIX 2.1 and TAXII 2.1 standards for structured threat intelligence exchange.
Standard Version Capabilities
STIX 2.1 Structured threat information representation
TAXII 2.1 Automated threat intelligence exchange
OpenIOC 1.1 Indicator of compromise representation
MISP Core Malware information sharing platform integration

1

Attribution Analysis

2

3

Predictive Analysis

Predict future threat actor behavior based on historical patterns and intelligence trends.
